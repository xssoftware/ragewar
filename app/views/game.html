Have you ever wondered what it's like to grow your own personal empire from the very first building blocks of the Stone Age? You now have the unique opportunity to lead a civilization on its wondrous adventure through time. Ragewar is a new generation strategy game, which gives you many choices of how you will progress from the cold and unfriendly Stone Age to the Awakening Era of science and reason.

Be a cunning warlord and explore the unique battle system, which surely will drive you to change your tactics even in battles. Or be a powerful shadow leader with vast economical resources and powerful friends which will make all your neighbors to worship you.
